Seunghoon Kim

I am a M.S. student at the CLVR Lab, part of the Graduate School of AI at KAIST, where I research on reinforcement learning and robotics. My M.S. advisor is Professor Joseph J. Lim.

From 2023 to 2025, I worked as an undergraduate research intern at Decision Making Lab at Seoul National University under supervision of Professor Min-hwan Oh. I primarily researched on decision making problems such as dynamic pricing and online matching bandits.
